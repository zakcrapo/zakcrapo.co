Zak is a visual designer working at IBM in Austin, Texas. He graduated from the Maryland Institute College of Art with a BFA in Graphic Design.

In his free time he runs the IBM Make Lab, a bite-sized print studio stuffed between the whiteboards of Austin's design studio.

Home in the Fall, Afterhours 2017


Summer 2017
18in × 24in
Edition of 44


Afterhours is an annual poster show where a portion of the earnings are donated to a local non-profit. This is my second year participating in the event and I was darn excited to bring the warm and cozy colors of a New England autumn to a home near you.
